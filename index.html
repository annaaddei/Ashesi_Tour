Administration Block

The Administrative Block, officially known as the Richadell Hall, houses most of the members of staff at Ashesi University, along with Faculty with the business and Art and Science Departments. This block is home to the Finance Department, the HR Department,the Admissions Departmet and the Office of Student and Community Affairs.

Engineering Block

The engineering block is the newest addition to Ashesi on the academic front. Arriving just in time to support the adoption of our first Engineering class, it houses valuable infrastructure needed for holistic education of all students. The Engineering block is home to the biggest congregational hall in Ashesi, the Norton-Motolsky Hall. When not in use, it provides two ultra-mordern classrooms where teaching and learning can take place flexibly with its easily movable chairs and tables.

Classrooms

At Ashesi University, classrooms are designed with the welfare of each student in mind. The small classrooms and thus class sizes make it possible for each student to get all the attention they need to excell. With each classroom fitted with a projector and sound system, lecturers have the flexibility to make their class sessions as dynamic as possible, in order to meet the needs of the diverse students with various learning styles.

Library

Ashesi has an ultra-mordern Library, that is well stocked with the latest resources across various topics and discourses. The library provides two conference rooms well furnished with a projector and board to facilitate meetings by students, staff and faculty. To enhance research, the library makes available e-library services and has computers, printers and photocopiers to enhance the academic experiences of students.

Cafeteria

They say a hungry man is an angry man. In order to ensure that all members of the Ashesi Community are kept sane, there are two cafeterias always ready to serve the community healthy and sumptious meals. The first is located in the main school block and is run by the administration whiles the other is run by the Student Council. The cafeterias work late hours and provide an array of meals from full meals to snacks and beverages, all available at affordable prices.
